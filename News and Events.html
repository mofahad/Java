One Day Workshop on Android Apps Development, Organized by ITSEC CSI Student Branch
_____

AUGUST 23, 2016



CSE Department, I.T.S Engineering College, organized one day Workshop on 'Android Application Development' by Mr. Devender Khari.

The opening of program was made by welcoming Mr. Devender Khari. Mr. Khari is a graduate in CSE from Shivaji University and Masters from BITS, Pilani. Currently he is working as CEO and Director of DKOP Labs Private Ltd. He has rich experience of over 18 years of working in companies like Cadence Design Systems and Bunka Orient India (Grape City).

The talk was initiated by Honourable guest, briefing about concepts of Core Java, followed by elaboration on activities, intents, different GUI elements using XML and android database SQLite.

The selected students of CSE 3rd year attended the workshop. Dr. Ashish Kumar, HOD-CSE, gave a Vote of Thanks and Token of Appreciation to Mr. Devender Khari.



INTER College Technical Fest | 'Saganika-2016'
_____

MARCH 17, 2016



CSE Department Society 'Avant Garde' organized a inter college Technical Fest 'SANGANIKA-2016' in association with Computer Society of India, Student Branch at I.T.S. Engineering College, Greater Noida.

Mr. Ankur Saxena, Convener, Avant Garde, SBC ITSEC CSI Student Branch and event coordinator of Sanganika 2016, welcomed Dr. Arun Sharma, Chairman-CSI Ghaziabad Chapter, Mr. Vikas Srivastava, Treasurer-CSI Ghaziabad Chapter & Mr. Rakesh Missra-Secretary Elet. CSI Ghaziabad Chapter.

The technical fest comprised of four events namely: WEB WAR, ITSEC-COC-2016, TECHNICAL QUIZ and TECHNO MASTI. There were more than 150 students who participated in various events from ITS Engineering College, GNIT, Galgotias’s College of Engineering & Technology, G.L Bajaj Inst. of Technology and Management and other colleges as well.

The objective of the festival was to create forum for the students comprising of all elements of experiential learning, to test their intellectual skills, simultaneously opening avenues for sharing mutual experience, skills and talent & to reach their collective and individual aspirations, in the field of technology and other activities on a common platform.

This festival would help in developing professional skills like leadership, team work, business acumen, resource generation & management among the students. Winners of all four events were given Cash prizes and certificates as follows:

Code Optimization Challenge: 1st Prize was won by Kirti and Ashmita of ITSEC, 2nd Prize was won by Shivam and Tejas of GL Bajaj Inst. of Technology and Management.

Web War: 1st Prize was won by Anubhav and Harsh of ITSEC, 2nd Prize was bagged by Nikhil and Rahul of ITSEC.

Techno Masti: 1st Prize was won by Vikrant and Priyank of ITSEC, 2nd Prize was bagged by Omkar and Vaibhav Satti of ITSEC.

Technical Quiz: 1st Prize was won by Sameer Khan and Sagar Goel of ITSEC, 2nd Prize was bagged by Shahbaz Haider and Parvaiz Khan of ITSEC respectively.

Finally, Mr. Ankur Saxena congratulated the winners and gave a Vote of Thanks Speech.
